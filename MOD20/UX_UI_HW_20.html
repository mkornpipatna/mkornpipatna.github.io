Introduction

What is One Minute Reader?

One Minute Reader is an in-browser web application for young kids to log into independently to practice their reading skills. This application was created as part of a larger program that was created for schools, but then was eventually made available to parents to use at home with their kids.

The application is a product of Read Naturally and the website that promotes it to parents was originally designed by program developers as part of a small effort, not part of a huge UX or UI design thinking cycle. My work to improve the One Minute Reader website involved working with SME's at all levels of the company as well as researching competitors and audiences for inspiration and direction.
